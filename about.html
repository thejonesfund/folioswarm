Our Story

Built by an insider.
For everyone else.

Folioswarm exists because the tools professional investors use have never made it to the people who need them most — everyday investors trying to make smart decisions with their own money.

Our Mission

Bridge the gap between complex data and confident decisions.

For decades, the gap between institutional and retail investing has been defined not by access to markets, but access to understanding. Professional money managers blend technical and fundamental analysis as a matter of routine. For most individual investors, that combination has remained out of reach — buried in jargon, scattered across tools, or locked behind expensive advisors.

Folioswarm changes that. We translate the same analytical frameworks used by professional portfolio managers into clear, visual intelligence that any investor can act on — in seconds, not hours.

The Founder
👤
Founder & Builder
Former Investment Executive · Folioswarm

After careers at Charles Schwab, Merrill Lynch, and Raymond James — managing money for individuals, businesses, and pension plans — I saw firsthand how differently professional investors approach decision-making compared to the people they serve.

I sold my practice in 2013 with one goal: build a platform that gives everyday investors the same analytical clarity that institutional managers take for granted. For years, Folioswarm was a passion project. When AI matured to the point where a single builder could create something genuinely powerful, I knew the time was right to build it lean and build it right.

Folioswarm is the tool I wish every client had walked in with.
